What We Do

Here at Red-X we are continuously, “REDEFINING EXCELLENCE”. RED-X is the largest and the only socio-adventure club of Manipal Academy of Higher Education which consists of two wings- The intrepid Adventure wing and the dutiful Disha wing. RED-X believes that our experiences make us who we are and thus, strives to make an eventful and pleasurable experience for each of its members.
REDX is MAHE’s official and the only socio-adventure club hoarding 7000+ active members Your one-stop destination for activities ranging from high altitude trekking to donation drives from triathlons to beach cleanliness drives, keeping our motto intact which would be REDEFINING EXCELLENCE. We strive to impact lives and ensure a better society while making the journey memorable and enlightening and this makes us the most lively club since 2004.

Adventure

REDX is an exclusive adventure seeking club that aims to assist all adrenaline junkies in their quest to pursue new heights and explore the unexplored. This wing organises thrilling and enticing events like the renowned Himalayan Treks, the Triathlon and the highly anticipated Sunburn festival.
However, the adventure does not stop there. Red-X also organizes intriguing online events such as Treasure Hunts and IPL Auctions.

Disha

Here, members are “directed” towards a committed and selfless path that seeks to bring about a change in the society by helping the weaker sections of society. This wing does the needful by organising the flavourful food fiesta, the highly contributing blood donation drives and purposeful awareness campaigns.
Despite the pandemic RED-X had successfully carried out a PAN-India cloth donation drive, thereby achieving the main goal of giving back to society.
